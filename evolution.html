This is my tribute to some of my favourite music legends - The Beatles, Elvis, Michael Jackson, Lady Gaga and Adele. With the 'Black and White' theme, I brought the Piano, the Abbey Road crossing, and the 'evolution' of all this together, in literal and figurative sense.

For the piano or the crossing base, I used a high res image of the front angle of a piano, and compressed and flattened the 3D aspects and saturated all colour or shadows. Then I appropriately placed the silhouettes in chronological order.
